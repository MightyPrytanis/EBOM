# EBOM Mandatory Additions v1.1

This document is a complete compilation of all mandatory additions to EBOM 1.0 specification.

## User-Developed Human Interaction Layer

### Sections A-H
- **Crisis Protocols (Corrected)**: Details regarding protocol corrections that enhance the user experience and safety.

## Critical Technical Gaps Identified in Analysis
- **Claim Classification**: Procedures and requirements for classifying claims.
- **Reasoning Verification**: Mechanisms to verify the reasoning behind claims.
- **Confidence Semantics**: The semantics that define the confidence levels for claims.
- **Temporal Policies**: Policies that govern time-sensitive claims and interactions.
- **Contradiction Resolution**: Strategies for resolving contradictions within claims.
- **Source Credibility**: Metrics and guidelines to assess the credibility of sources.
- **User Correction Mechanisms**: Mechanisms empowering users to correct erroneous claims or interactions.
- **Adversarial Test Expansion**: Plans for expanding adversarial testing to cover more scenarios.
